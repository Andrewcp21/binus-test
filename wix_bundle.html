Curriculum Overview

A 12-Week, Applied Learning Journey, crafted by the faculty of the #1 University in Indonesia.

Phase 1 Week 1 - 2

Problem Framing

Data-driven decision making and AI strategy.

Move from a tech-talent mindset to a strategic problem-solver. This phase teaches you how to identify high-value business problems and frame them through the lens of data and AI.

  • Understand the impact of Digital Transformation and AI on modern industries.
  • Master problem-solving frameworks like the 5 Whys and Hypothesis-driven thinking.
  • Learn to translate open-ended business challenges into measurable data metrics.
  • Build an AI-first mindset for developing business solutions and project roadmaps.
Phase 2 Week 3 - 4

Data Intelligence

Mastering EDA and visualization to find the "Why."

Before you can use AI, you must understand your data. This phase focuses on the foundations of data quality, exploratory analysis (EDA), and the art of data storytelling.

  • Master Data Fundamentals: Differentiate between structured/unstructured data and ensure data quality.
  • Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using descriptive statistics to find hidden trends.
  • Learn Data Visualization principles to turn complex numbers into clear, actionable insights.
  • Develop Data Storytelling skills to present findings that influence stakeholders.
Phase 3 Week 5 - 7

Generative AI Mastery

Prompt engineering and building Custom AI Assistants with ChatGPT.

Transition from using AI as a search engine to using it as a specialized partner. Learn the mechanics of LLMs and build custom, no-code AI assistants tailored to specific business needs.

  • Master Prompt Engineering (101 & Advanced) for marketing, operations, and HR.
  • Build Custom No-Code AI Chatbots and assistants for specialized business functions.
  • Understand the Ethics of AI: Navigating bias, hallucination, and data privacy.
  • Explore Applied AI Analytics use cases to automate data-heavy tasks.
Phase 4 Week 8 - 9

Agentic Automation

Connecting AI to the real world with n8n and API integrations.

Take your AI assistants beyond the chat box. Learn to build autonomous "agents" that can trigger workflows, talk to external apps via APIs, and execute tasks without manual intervention.

  • Learn Workflow Automation basics using n8n to connect disparate business tools.
  • Implement API Integrations to allow your AI to read and write data to the real world.
  • Design Multi-Agent Systems where different AI agents collaborate on complex workflows.
  • Build "Human-in-the-loop" systems to ensure AI automation remains reliable and safe.
Phase 5 Week 10 - 12

Capstone Residency

Building, refining, and presenting your final solution.

The culmination of your learning. In this residency, you will build a fully functional AI-driven solution, receive expert mentorship, and pitch your project to an industry panel.

  • AI-First UX/UI Design: Learn patterns for AI interfaces (dashboards, triage inboxes, etc.).
  • Intensive Mentorship: Refine your technical architecture and automation stability with experts.
  • Pitching & Presentation: Master the framework of Problem → Data → Insight → Recommendation.
  • Final Demo Day: Present your working prototype and business case to external panelists.
